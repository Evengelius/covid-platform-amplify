COVID-19 treatments & vaccines
Experimental Unapproved Treatments for COVID-19
# Name Description
1 Ritonavir An HIV protease inhibitor used in combination with other antivirals in the treatment of HIV infection.
2 Chloroquine An antimalarial drug used to treat susceptible infections with P. vivax, P. malariae, P. ovale, and P. falciparum. It is also used for second line treatment for rheumatoid arthritis.
3 AstraZeneca COVID-19 Vaccine A two-dose replication-deficient adenovirus vector vaccine used to prevent COVID-19 caused by SARS-CoV-2.
4 Lopinavir An HIV-1 protease inhibitor used in combination with ritonavir to treat human immunodeficiency virus (HIV) infection.
5 Moderna COVID-19 Vaccine An mRNA vaccine for the prevention of COVID-19, the disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus, in patients aged 18 years and older.
6 Favipiravir An antiviral used to manage influenza, and that has the potential to target other viral infections.
7 Umifenovir A dual direct-acting antiviral/host-targeting agent used in the treatment and prophylaxis of influenza and other respiratory viruses.

12 Human interferon beta A polypeptide drug used in the treatment of relapsing forms of Multiple Sclerosis (MS).
13 Darunavir A HIV protease inhibitor used in the treatment of human immunodeficiency virus (HIV) infection in patients with history of prior antiretroviral therapies.
14 Fingolimod A sphingosine 1-phosphate receptor modulator used to treat patients with the relapsing-remitting form of multiple sclerosis (MS) and studied to manage lung complications of COVID-19.
15 Triazavirin An antiviral used to treat several strains of influenza.
